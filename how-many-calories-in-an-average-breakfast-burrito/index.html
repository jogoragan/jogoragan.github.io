How Many Calories in an Average Breakfast Burrito?

The calorie count of a breakfast burrito can vary widely depending on the ingredients used. On average, a breakfast burrito contains around 300-500 calories. However, some burritos can contain upwards of 1,000 calories!

The calorie count of your breakfast burrito will depend on factors such as:

  • The size of the tortilla
  • The type and amount of protein (e.g. bacon, sausage, eggs)
  • The type and amount of cheese
  • The presence of high-calorie condiments (e.g. sour cream, guacamole)

Ingredients That Affect Calorie Count

If you're trying to keep your calorie count in check, there are a few ingredients to be mindful of:

  • Cheese: Cheese is a high-calorie ingredient that can quickly add up. Opt for a small amount of a lower-calorie cheese like feta or goat cheese instead of a large amount of cheddar or Monterey Jack.
  • Meat: Breakfast meats like bacon and sausage are high in calories and fat. Consider using a leaner protein like turkey bacon or chicken sausage.
  • Tortilla: The size of your tortilla can make a big difference in calorie count. Opt for a smaller tortilla or use a lettuce wrap instead.
  • Condiments: High-calorie condiments like sour cream and guacamole can add a lot of calories. Consider using salsa or hot sauce instead.

How to Make Healthier Breakfast Burritos

If you're looking to make a healthier breakfast burrito, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Use a smaller tortilla
  • Choose a lean protein like turkey bacon or chicken sausage
  • Use a lower-calorie cheese like feta or goat cheese
  • Add lots of veggies like peppers, onions, and spinach
  • Use salsa or hot sauce instead of high-calorie condiments

Step-by-Step Guide to Making a Healthy Breakfast Burrito

Here's a simple recipe for a healthy breakfast burrito:

  1. Scramble two eggs in a nonstick pan with a bit of cooking spray.
  2. Add diced peppers, onions, and spinach to the pan and cook until the veggies are soft.
  3. Warm a small tortilla in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.
  4. Spread a small amount of salsa on the tortilla.
  5. Add the egg and veggie mixture to the tortilla.
  6. Top with a small amount of crumbled feta cheese.
  7. Roll the tortilla into a burrito and enjoy!

Tips for Choosing a Healthy Breakfast Burrito

When you're out and about and need to grab a quick breakfast burrito, here are a few tips for choosing a healthier option:

  • Look for burritos that are made with whole wheat tortillas.
  • Choose a burrito with plenty of veggies.
  • Opt for a lean protein like chicken or turkey.
  • Avoid high-calorie condiments like sour cream and guacamole.
  • Ask for the cheese to be left off or only added in small amounts.

FAQs

Q: Are breakfast burritos healthy?

A: Breakfast burritos can be healthy if they're made with the right ingredients. Look for burritos that are made with whole wheat tortillas, plenty of veggies, and lean proteins.

Q: How many calories are in a breakfast burrito from McDonald's?

A: A breakfast burrito from McDonald's contains around 300-400 calories.

Q: Are breakfast burritos high in fat?

A: Breakfast burritos can be high in fat depending on the ingredients used. Look for lean proteins and use cheese and condiments in moderation.

Pros and Cons of Eating Breakfast Burritos

Pros:

  • Quick and convenient breakfast option
  • Can be a good source of protein and veggies
  • Can be a filling and satisfying meal

Cons:

  • Can be high in calories and fat
  • Some burritos contain a lot of sodium
  • May not be the healthiest breakfast option

Overall, breakfast burritos can be a delicious and satisfying breakfast option. Just be mindful of the ingredients you're using and aim for a balance of protein, veggies, and healthy fats.
