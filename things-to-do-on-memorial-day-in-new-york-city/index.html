Memorial Day is a time to honor the fallen heroes who have served the United States military. Memorial Day is observed on the last Monday of May every year, and it is a day to remember the brave men and women who gave their lives in service to their country. In New York City, there are many things to do on Memorial Day. In this article, we will discuss some of the best things to do in New York City on Memorial Day.

1. Attend the Fleet Week Parade

New York City is home to one of the largest Fleet Weeks in the country. Fleet Week is a celebration of the United States Navy, Coast Guard, and Marine Corps. Each year, the Fleet Week Parade takes place on Memorial Day. The parade starts at 11:30 am in Battery Park and makes its way up 5th Avenue. This is a great way to honor our military and show your support for our troops.

2. Visit the Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum

The Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum is a great place to visit on Memorial Day. The museum is located on the West Side of Manhattan and is home to the USS Intrepid aircraft carrier. The museum has many exhibits and interactive displays that showcase the history of the military. This is a great way to learn about the sacrifices that our military has made for our country.

3. Take a Walk through Central Park

Central Park is a great place to spend Memorial Day. The park is located in the heart of Manhattan and is home to many memorials and monuments. You can take a walk through the park and visit the Strawberry Fields Memorial, which is a tribute to John Lennon. This is a great way to honor the fallen heroes who have served our country.

4. Attend the National Memorial Day Concert

The National Memorial Day Concert is an annual event that takes place on the West Lawn of the United States Capitol in Washington D.C. The concert is broadcast live on PBS and is a great way to honor the fallen heroes who have served our country. You can attend the concert in person or watch it on television.

5. Visit the 9/11 Memorial and Museum

The 9/11 Memorial and Museum is a great place to visit on Memorial Day. The memorial is located at the World Trade Center site and is a tribute to the victims of the 9/11 terrorist attacks. The museum has many exhibits and interactive displays that showcase the history of the attacks. This is a great way to honor the fallen heroes who lost their lives on that tragic day.

6. Attend the Memorial Day Parade

The Memorial Day Parade is an annual event that takes place in many cities across the United States. In New York City, the parade starts at 11 am on Memorial Day and makes its way up Broadway. This is a great way to show your support for our troops and honor the fallen heroes who have served our country.

7. Visit the Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a great place to visit on Memorial Day. The statue is located on Liberty Island and is a symbol of freedom and democracy. You can take a ferry to the island and visit the statue and the museum. This is a great way to honor the fallen heroes who have served our country.

8. Attend a Memorial Day Concert

Many cities across the United States host Memorial Day concerts. In New York City, there are many concerts that take place on Memorial Day. You can attend a concert at Carnegie Hall or Lincoln Center. This is a great way to honor the fallen heroes who have served our country.

9. Visit the American Museum of Natural History

The American Museum of Natural History is a great place to visit on Memorial Day. The museum has many exhibits and interactive displays that showcase the history of the United States. You can learn about the sacrifices that our military has made for our country. This is a great way to honor the fallen heroes who have served our country.

10. Take a Cruise on the Hudson River

Taking a cruise on the Hudson River is a great way to spend Memorial Day. You can take a cruise down the river and see many of the memorials and monuments that honor the fallen heroes who have served our country. This is a great way to show your support for our troops and honor the sacrifices that they have made for our country.

Conclusion

Memorial Day is a time to honor the fallen heroes who have served the United States military. In New York City, there are many things to do on Memorial Day. You can attend the Fleet Week Parade, visit the Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum, take a walk through Central Park, attend the National Memorial Day Concert, visit the 9/11 Memorial and Museum, attend the Memorial Day Parade, visit the Statue of Liberty, attend a Memorial Day concert, visit the American Museum of Natural History, or take a cruise on the Hudson River. These are all great ways to honor the fallen heroes who have served our country.
