Treadmill

A treadmill is a great option for those who want to lose weight. It is a low-impact exercise that can be done indoors, making it perfect for those who live in areas with inclement weather. Treadmills come in different sizes and styles, so you can choose one that fits your needs and budget.

Pros:

  • Low-impact exercise
  • Indoor exercise
  • Customizable settings

Cons:

  • Expensive
  • Takes up a lot of space

Elliptical

An elliptical is another low-impact exercise that can be done indoors. It is a great option for those who want a full-body workout without putting too much pressure on their joints. Ellipticals come in different sizes and styles, so you can choose one that fits your needs and budget.

Pros:

  • Low-impact exercise
  • Full-body workout
  • Indoor exercise

Cons:

  • Expensive
  • Takes up a lot of space
