If you're planning to spend your Labor Day weekend in Memphis, then you're in luck! The city is bustling with events and activities that will keep you entertained throughout the long weekend. From live music to outdoor adventures, there's something for everyone. In this article, we'll list some of the best things to do in Memphis Labor Day weekend 2022.

Table of Contents

  • Live Music
  • Outdoor Adventures
  • Food Festivals

Live Music

Memphis is known for its vibrant music scene, and Labor Day weekend is no exception. There are several music festivals and concerts happening throughout the city, including the Memphis Music & Heritage Festival, which features a variety of musical genres and local artists. The Beale Street Jazz Festival is another popular event that showcases jazz and blues musicians from around the country.

If you're looking for a more intimate setting, check out the Levitt Shell's free outdoor concerts in Overton Park. They have a diverse lineup of musicians, including local acts and nationally recognized artists.

Outdoor Adventures

Memphis has plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities during Labor Day weekend. Take a stroll through the beautiful Memphis Botanic Garden or hike the trails at Shelby Farms Park. If you're feeling adventurous, try kayaking or paddleboarding on the Mississippi River.

For a unique experience, head to the Memphis Zoo for their Sunset Safari, where you can enjoy live music and animal encounters after hours.

Food Festivals

Memphis is known for its delicious food, and Labor Day weekend is the perfect time to indulge in some local cuisine. The Memphis Food & Wine Festival is a must-visit event, featuring top chefs and wineries from around the country.

The World Championship BBQ Cooking Contest is another popular food festival, where teams from around the world compete for the title of best barbecue. You can sample some of the best barbecue in the world and enjoy live music and entertainment.

Art Exhibitions

If you're an art lover, then you'll enjoy the many exhibitions and galleries that Memphis has to offer. The Brooks Museum of Art is hosting several exhibitions during Labor Day weekend, including a showcase of contemporary Southern artists.

The Metal Museum is also worth a visit, with its collection of metalwork and sculpture. You can even take a blacksmithing class if you're feeling creative!

Sports Events

Sports fans will have plenty to do during Labor Day weekend in Memphis. The Memphis Redbirds, the city's minor league baseball team, will have games at AutoZone Park throughout the weekend.

The Memphis Grizzlies, the city's NBA team, will also have preseason games at FedExForum. You can catch some of the best basketball players in the world as they prepare for the upcoming season.

Conclusion

Memphis is a vibrant city with plenty of things to do during Labor Day weekend. Whether you're interested in music, food, art, or sports, there's something for everyone. Plan your itinerary in advance to make the most of your trip, and enjoy all that Memphis has to offer!
