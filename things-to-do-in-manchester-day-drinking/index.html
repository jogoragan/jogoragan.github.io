Things To Do In Manchester Day Drinking A Guide To An Unforgettable Experience

Are you looking for a fun way to spend your day in Manchester? Why not try day drinking? Manchester is a vibrant city with a lot of exciting things to do and see, and adding day drinking to your itinerary can make your experience even more memorable. In this article, we’ll share with you some of the best things to do in Manchester day drinking, as well as tips to make the most of your experience.

Bar Crawl

One of the best ways to experience Manchester day drinking is by going on a bar crawl. Manchester is home to a diverse range of bars, from traditional pubs to trendy cocktail bars. You can start your bar crawl in the Northern Quarter, which is known for its independent bars and live music venues. From there, you can head to Deansgate or Spinningfields, which are home to some of the city’s best cocktail bars.

Rooftop Bars

If you’re looking for a unique Manchester day drinking experience, you can head to one of the city’s rooftop bars. These bars offer stunning views of the city skyline and are perfect for a sunny day. Some of the best rooftop bars in Manchester include 20 Stories, Cloud 23, and The Ivy. Make sure to book in advance, as these bars can get crowded.

Beer Gardens

Manchester has a great selection of beer gardens, which are perfect for a relaxed day drinking experience. You can head to The Oast House, which is located in Spinningfields and has a large outdoor area with live music. Another great beer garden is The Wharf, which is located on the Canal Street and offers stunning views of the water.

Distillery Tours

If you’re interested in learning about the distilling process, you can go on a distillery tour. Manchester is home to a few distilleries that offer tours and tastings. You can visit the Manchester Gin Distillery, which is located in the city center and offers a range of gin tasting experiences. Another great option is the Three Rivers Distillery, which is located in Ancoats and specializes in small-batch gin.

Outdoor Activities

Manchester has a lot of outdoor activities that you can enjoy while day drinking. You can take a stroll in Heaton Park, which is the largest park in Manchester and has a lake and a golf course. Another great option is the Fletcher Moss Botanical Gardens, which is located in Didsbury and has a range of exotic plants.

Pub Food

Manchester is known for its pub culture, and you can’t go wrong with pub food while day drinking. You can head to The Marble Arch, which is located in the city center and is known for its homemade pies. Another great option is The Briton’s Protection, which is a traditional pub that serves classic British dishes.

Live Music

If you’re a music lover, you can enjoy live music while day drinking. Manchester is home to a vibrant music scene, and you can find live music in many bars and venues. You can head to Night & Day Café, which is located in the Northern Quarter and is known for its indie music. Another great option is Band on the Wall, which is a legendary music venue that has hosted many famous artists.

Shopping

If you’re in the mood for some shopping, you can visit Manchester’s many shopping streets and centers. You can head to the Arndale Centre, which is the largest shopping center in Manchester and has a range of high street and designer stores. Another great option is the Northern Quarter, which is known for its independent shops and boutiques.

Art Galleries

Manchester has a thriving art scene, and you can visit some of the city’s many galleries while day drinking. You can head to the Manchester Art Gallery, which has a collection of more than 25,000 objects that span six centuries. Another great option is the Whitworth Art Gallery, which is located in a park and has a range of contemporary art exhibitions.

Museums

Manchester has a range of museums that you can visit while day drinking, including the Museum of Science and Industry and the National Football Museum. These museums offer a unique experience and are perfect for a rainy day.

Tips for Manchester Day Drinking

Here are some tips to make the most of your Manchester day drinking experience:

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Plan your itinerary in advance to make the most of your time.
  • Use public transport or hire a taxi to get around safely.
  • Try different drinks and cocktails to discover new flavors.
  • Don’t forget to eat to avoid getting too drunk.

Conclusion

Manchester is a great city to explore while day drinking. Whether you’re interested in bars, rooftop views, outdoor activities, or cultural experiences, there’s something for everyone. By following our tips and recommendations, you can make the most of your Manchester day drinking experience and create unforgettable memories.
