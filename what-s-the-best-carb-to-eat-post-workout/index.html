In this article, we will be discussing what type of carbohydrates are best to consume after a workout to help your body recover and build muscle. We will also be including tips and solutions to help you make the most out of your post-workout meal. Below is a table of content to help you navigate through the article.

Description:

Carbohydrates are an important macronutrient that our bodies need to function properly. After a workout, our bodies need to replenish the glycogen stores that have been depleted during exercise. Consuming carbohydrates post workout helps to restore glycogen levels, which helps with muscle recovery and growth. The type of carbohydrate you consume post workout is also important. The best carb to eat post workout is one that is easily digestible, low in fiber, and has a high glycemic index.

How to:

The best way to consume carbohydrates post workout is within 30 minutes to an hour after exercising. This is when your body is most receptive to absorbing nutrients. Aim for 20-30 grams of carbohydrates post workout. You can consume carbohydrates in the form of food or drink. Some examples of post-workout carbohydrates include:

  • White rice
  • White bread
  • Potatoes
  • Fruit juice
  • Dried fruit
  • Sports drinks

Step by step:

Here are the steps to follow when consuming carbohydrates post workout:

  1. Choose a carbohydrate that is easily digestible and has a high glycemic index
  2. Consume the carbohydrate within 30 minutes to an hour after exercising
  3. Aim for 20-30 grams of carbohydrates post workout
  4. Pair the carbohydrate with a source of protein for optimal muscle recovery and growth

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you make the most out of your post-workout meal:

  • Prep your post-workout meal before exercising to ensure you have a quick and easy option to consume after your workout
  • Choose a carbohydrate that you enjoy consuming to make sure you stick to your post-workout routine
  • If you are on a low-carb diet, consider adding in carbohydrates post workout to aid in muscle recovery and growth
