What Is The Best Exercise To Get Rid Of Love Handles

Written by Alvine Apr 24, 2023 · 4 min read

.

If you're looking to get rid of those stubborn love handles, you're not alone. Love handles are the excess fat that accumulates around the waistline and can be difficult to get rid of. While there is no magic formula that can guarantee instant results, there are exercises that can help you achieve your goal. In this post, we will explore the best exercise to do to get rid of love handles, along with tips, solutions, and FAQs to help you get started.

What are Love Handles?

Love handles are the excess fat that accumulates around the waistline. They are also known as muffin tops, and can be difficult to get rid of. Love handles are often caused by a combination of factors, including genetics, poor diet, and lack of exercise. They can be a source of frustration for many people, and can often lead to self-consciousness and low self-esteem.

Solution to Get Rid of Love Handles

The best way to get rid of love handles is to adopt a healthy lifestyle. This includes eating a balanced diet, getting regular exercise, and getting enough sleep. While there is no magic formula that can guarantee instant results, there are exercises that can help you achieve your goal.

Best Exercise to Get Rid of Love Handles

The best exercise to get rid of love handles is the side plank. This exercise targets the oblique muscles, which are the muscles that run along the sides of your waist. The side plank is a simple exercise that can be done anywhere, and requires no equipment. It is also an effective exercise for improving core strength and stability.

How to Do the Best Exercise for Love Handles

To do the side plank:
  1. Start by lying on your side, with your elbow directly beneath your shoulder.
  2. Place your feet together and lift your hips off the ground, creating a straight line from your head to your feet.
  3. Hold this position for 30 seconds, or as long as you can.
  4. Repeat on the other side.

Tips to Maximize Results

To maximize the results of the side plank:
  • Engage your core by pulling your belly button towards your spine.
  • Keep your body in a straight line from your head to your feet.
  • Don't let your hips sag or drop.
  • Breathe deeply throughout the exercise.

FAQs

Q: How often should I do the side plank?
A: Aim to do the side plank 3-4 times per week, for 30-60 seconds each time. Q: Will the side plank help me lose weight?
A: The side plank can help tone your oblique muscles and improve your overall core strength, but it is not a weight loss exercise. To lose weight, you need to adopt a healthy lifestyle that includes exercise and a balanced diet.

Pros and Cons

Pros:
  • The side plank is a simple exercise that can be done anywhere, and requires no equipment.
  • It is an effective exercise for toning the oblique muscles, improving core strength and stability, and reducing the appearance of love handles.
Cons:
  • The side plank can be challenging for beginners, and may require some practice to master.
  • It is not a weight loss exercise, and should be combined with a healthy lifestyle to achieve optimal results.
In conclusion, the best exercise to do to get rid of love handles is the side plank. This exercise targets the oblique muscles, which are the muscles that run along the sides of your waist, and can help reduce the appearance of love handles. To maximize the results of the side plank, be sure to engage your core, keep your body in a straight line, and breathe deeply throughout the exercise. Remember to adopt a healthy lifestyle that includes exercise and a balanced diet to achieve optimal results.
