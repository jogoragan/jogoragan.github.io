Is It Better To Go For A Walk In The Morning Or Evening

If you're someone who loves to go for a walk, you might wonder whether it's better to do it in the morning or evening. The truth is, both have their advantages and disadvantages. In this article, we'll explore the benefits and drawbacks of walking in the morning and evening, and hopefully, help you decide what works best for you.

Benefits of Walking in the Morning

Walking in the morning has several benefits. Firstly, it helps to kickstart your metabolism, which means that you'll burn more calories throughout the day. Secondly, it can help you feel more energized and alert, making it easier to focus on tasks at work or home. Lastly, walking in the morning gives you a chance to enjoy the fresh air and peacefulness of the early morning, which can be a great way to start your day.

Drawbacks of Walking in the Morning

The main drawback of walking in the morning is that it can be difficult to wake up early, especially if you're not a morning person. Additionally, the weather can be unpredictable, and it's not always easy to get motivated to go for a walk when it's cold or rainy outside.

Advantages of Walking in the Evening

Walking in the evening also has its advantages. Firstly, it can be a great way to unwind after a long day at work, and it can help you to de-stress and relax. Secondly, the weather tends to be more pleasant in the evening, which can make it more enjoyable to go for a walk. Lastly, if you have trouble sleeping, walking in the evening can help you to feel more tired and ready for bed.

Disadvantages of Walking in the Evening

The main disadvantage of walking in the evening is that it can interfere with your sleep if you do it too close to bedtime. Additionally, if you live in a busy area, it can be difficult to find a peaceful place to walk in the evening. Lastly, if you're someone who tends to get tired in the evening, it can be challenging to motivate yourself to go for a walk after a long day.

How to Start Walking in the Morning/Evening

If you're new to walking, it can be challenging to know where to start. Here are some tips:

  1. Start small: Begin with short walks and gradually increase the distance as you become more comfortable.
  2. Find a buddy: Walking with a friend or family member can make it more fun and keep you accountable.
  3. Set a goal: Decide on a specific goal, such as walking for 30 minutes a day, and work towards it.
  4. Track your progress: Use a fitness tracker or app to track your steps and monitor your progress.

Tips for a Successful Walk

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your walks:

  • Dress appropriately: Wear comfortable clothes and shoes that are suitable for the weather.
  • Stay hydrated: Bring water with you, especially if you're walking in the heat.
  • Listen to music or a podcast: This can help to make your walk more enjoyable.
  • Take breaks: If you're feeling tired, take a break and sit down for a few minutes.

FAQs

Q: Is it better to walk on an empty stomach?

A: It's generally recommended to eat something light before going for a walk, such as a piece of fruit or a granola bar. This will help to give you energy and prevent you from feeling lightheaded or dizzy.

Q: How long should I walk for?

A: Aim for at least 30 minutes of walking per day, but it's fine to start with shorter walks and gradually increase the time as you become more comfortable.

Q: Is it okay to walk every day?

A: Yes, it's generally safe to walk every day, as long as you build up your distance and intensity gradually and listen to your body.

Conclusion

Ultimately, whether you choose to go for a walk in the morning or evening depends on your personal preferences and lifestyle. Both have their advantages and disadvantages, so it's important to find a time that works best for you. Remember to start small, set goals, and track your progress to help you stay motivated and get the most out of your walks.
