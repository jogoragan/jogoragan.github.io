4 Day Gym Workout Routine For Weight Loss

If you are looking to lose weight, hitting the gym is the best way to achieve your goal. A 4 day gym workout routine for weight loss can help you burn calories, build muscle, and increase your metabolism. This article will outline an effective gym workout routine for weight loss, including exercises, sets, and reps.

Description

A 4 day gym workout routine for weight loss involves working out four days a week, with three days of rest in between. This routine is designed to help you lose weight by burning calories, building muscle, and increasing your metabolism. The workouts should be intense, with a focus on compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once.

How to

Before starting any gym workout routine, it is important to warm up properly to avoid injury. Spend 5-10 minutes on a treadmill or stationary bike to get your heart rate up and your muscles warm. Once you are warmed up, begin your workout with compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once. These exercises include squats, lunges, deadlifts, and bench presses.

Monday: Upper Body Workout

1. Bench press: 3 sets of 12 reps
2. Dumbbell rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
3. Overhead press: 3 sets of 12 reps
4. Lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 12 reps
5. Bicep curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
6. Tricep extensions: 3 sets of 12 reps

Tuesday: Lower Body Workout

1. Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
2. Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps
3. Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps
4. Leg press: 3 sets of 12 reps
5. Calf raises: 3 sets of 12 reps

Thursday: Cardio Day

1. Treadmill: 30 minutes
2. Stationary bike: 30 minutes
3. Stair stepper: 30 minutes
4. Elliptical: 30 minutes

Friday: Full Body Workout

1. Bench press: 3 sets of 12 reps
2. Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
3. Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps
4. Overhead press: 3 sets of 12 reps
5. Lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 12 reps
6. Bicep curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
7. Tricep extensions: 3 sets of 12 reps
8. Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps
9. Leg press: 3 sets of 12 reps
10. Calf raises: 3 sets of 12 reps

Step by Step

1. Warm up for 5-10 minutes on a treadmill or stationary bike. 2. Monday: Upper body workout, including bench press, dumbbell rows, overhead press, lat pulldowns, bicep curls, and tricep extensions. 3. Tuesday: Lower body workout, including squats, deadlifts, lunges, leg press, and calf raises. 4. Thursday: Cardio day, including 30 minutes on a treadmill, stationary bike, stair stepper, and elliptical. 5. Friday: Full body workout, including bench press, squats, deadlifts, overhead press, lat pulldowns, bicep curls, tricep extensions, lunges, leg press, and calf raises. 6. Rest on Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday.

Tips

1. Focus on compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once. 2. Use heavy weights and low reps to build muscle and increase your metabolism. 3. Incorporate cardio into your routine to burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health. 4. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout your workout. 5. Eat a healthy diet that is high in protein, complex carbs, and healthy fats to fuel your workouts and aid in weight loss.

Solution

A 4 day gym workout routine for weight loss is an effective way to burn calories, build muscle, and increase your metabolism. By incorporating compound exercises, heavy weights, and cardio into your routine, you can achieve your weight loss goals and improve your overall health.
