Heading to Las Vegas for Memorial Day weekend? You're in for a treat! This iconic city is a hub of entertainment, gambling, nightlife, and fine dining, making it the perfect destination for a long weekend getaway. In this article, we'll show you what to do in Vegas Memorial Weekend, from the best parties to the top attractions, and everything in between. Let's dive in!

Parties and Nightlife

Memorial Day weekend in Vegas is notorious for its wild parties and non-stop nightlife. Some of the city's top nightclubs and pool parties host special events and performances during this time, making it the perfect opportunity to dance the night away. Here are some of the best parties to check out:

1. Electric Daisy Carnival

This annual electronic dance music festival takes place over Memorial Day weekend and attracts thousands of music lovers from around the world. With multiple stages, carnival rides, and jaw-dropping performances, EDC is a must-see event for anyone visiting Vegas during this time.

2. Marquee Dayclub

Located at the Cosmopolitan, Marquee Dayclub is one of the city's top pool parties. During Memorial Day weekend, they host special events featuring world-famous DJs and performers. Don't miss out on the fun!

3. Omnia Nightclub

Omnia is a multi-level nightclub located at Caesars Palace, known for its state-of-the-art sound system and impressive light shows. During Memorial Day weekend, they host special events featuring some of the biggest names in the music industry.

Top Attractions

Aside from the parties and nightlife, Vegas has plenty of attractions to keep you entertained during Memorial Day weekend. From world-class shows to iconic landmarks, here are some of the top things to do:

1. Bellagio Fountains

The Bellagio Fountains are a must-see attraction in Vegas, featuring a choreographed water show set to music and lights. Shows take place every 30 minutes during the day and every 15 minutes at night.

2. High Roller Observation Wheel

This giant observation wheel located at the LINQ Promenade offers stunning views of the city from 550 feet in the air. It's the perfect way to take in the sights and sounds of Vegas.

3. Cirque du Soleil Shows

Vegas is known for its world-class entertainment, and Cirque du Soleil is one of the city's top attractions. With multiple shows to choose from, you're sure to be amazed by their acrobatic feats and stunning performances.

Fine Dining and Food

Vegas is home to some of the world's best restaurants and chefs, making it the perfect destination for foodies. From Michelin-starred fine dining to casual eateries, here are some of the top places to eat during Memorial Day weekend:

1. Joel Robuchon

This three-Michelin-starred restaurant located at the MGM Grand offers a decadent dining experience featuring modern French cuisine and impeccable service.

2. Momofuku

Located at the Cosmopolitan, Momofuku is a must-visit for lovers of Asian cuisine. Their menu features a mix of traditional and modern dishes, including their famous pork buns and ramen.

3. Gordon Ramsay Steak

Located at Paris Las Vegas, Gordon Ramsay Steak offers a classic steakhouse experience with a modern twist. Their menu features a variety of cuts of steak, as well as seafood and vegetarian options.

Outdoor Activities

If you're looking to get outside and enjoy the sunshine during Memorial Day weekend, Vegas has plenty of outdoor activities to choose from. From hiking to pool parties, here are some of the top options:

1. Red Rock Canyon

This stunning national conservation area located just outside of Vegas offers miles of hiking trails, rock climbing, and scenic drives. It's the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and reconnect with nature.

2. Tao Beach Club

Located at the Venetian, Tao Beach Club is one of the city's top pool parties. With a luxurious atmosphere and world-class DJs, it's the perfect place to relax and soak up the sun.

3. Grand Canyon Helicopter Tour

If you're looking for a once-in-a-lifetime experience, a helicopter tour of the Grand Canyon is a must. You'll soar over the majestic canyon and take in breathtaking views from above.

Tips for Memorial Day Weekend in Vegas

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your Memorial Day weekend in Vegas:

  • Book your hotel and activities in advance to avoid long lines and sold-out events.
  • Stay hydrated and wear sunscreen to avoid getting sunburned or dehydrated.
  • Take advantage of ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft to avoid traffic and parking hassles.
  • Be prepared to wait in long lines and crowds, especially at popular attractions and events.
  • Remember to gamble responsibly and set a budget for yourself before hitting the casino.

FAQs

Q: What is the weather like in Vegas during Memorial Day weekend?

A: Vegas can get quite hot during Memorial Day weekend, with temperatures averaging in the 90s. Be sure to stay hydrated and wear sunscreen if you plan on spending time outdoors.

Q: What should I wear to the parties and nightclubs?

A: Dress codes vary by venue, but most nightclubs and pool parties require upscale attire. For men, this typically means collared shirts and dress shoes. For women, cocktail dresses or stylish swimsuits are appropriate.

Q: Can I bring outside food or drinks to the pool parties?

A: Outside food and drinks are not allowed at most pool parties in Vegas. Be sure to check with the venue before arriving.

Now that you know what to do in Vegas Memorial Weekend, it's time to start planning your trip. Whether you're looking to party the night away, explore the city's top attractions, or indulge in world-class cuisine, Vegas has something for everyone. Have a great trip!
