If you’re someone who loves to work out in the morning, you know that it’s important to fuel your body properly before hitting the gym. But what should you drink before a morning workout? In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about pre-workout drinks, including the benefits, how to make them, and some delicious recipes to try out.

  • Benefits of Pre-Workout Drinks

Benefits of Pre-Workout Drinks

A pre-workout drink can provide numerous benefits for your workout, including:

  • Increased energy and focus
  • Improved endurance
  • Reduced muscle soreness
  • Increased muscle growth and repair
  • Improved hydration

By drinking a pre-workout drink, you can help your body perform at its best during your workout.

How to Make a Pre-Workout Drink

Making a pre-workout drink is easy and can be customized to your liking. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Choose a liquid base, such as water, coconut water, or almond milk.
  2. Add a source of carbohydrates, such as fruit or honey.
  3. Add a source of protein, such as protein powder or Greek yogurt.
  4. Consider adding a source of caffeine, such as coffee or green tea, for an extra boost.
  5. Blend all ingredients together and enjoy!

It’s important to note that you should drink your pre-workout drink about 30 minutes before your workout to give your body time to digest.

Delicious Pre-Workout Drink Recipes to Try

Green Smoothie

This green smoothie is packed with nutrients to fuel your workout.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp honey

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!

Chocolate Peanut Butter Shake

This delicious shake is perfect for those who love chocolate and peanut butter.

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I drink before a morning workout?

It’s recommended to drink a pre-workout drink that includes a source of carbohydrates, protein, and caffeine. This can include a smoothie made with fruit, protein powder, and coffee, or a sports drink that includes these ingredients.

How much should I drink before a morning workout?

You should aim to drink about 16-20 ounces of fluid before your workout. This can vary depending on your body weight and the length and intensity of your workout.

Can I drink coffee before a morning workout?

Yes, coffee can be a great source of caffeine before a morning workout. Just be sure to not overdo it and drink too much caffeine, as this can lead to jitters and anxiety.

The Pros and Cons of Pre-Workout Drinks

Pros:

  • Increased energy and focus
  • Improved endurance
  • Reduced muscle soreness
  • Increased muscle growth and repair
  • Improved hydration

Cons:

  • Some pre-workout drinks can be high in sugar and calories
  • Some people may experience negative side effects from caffeine, such as jitters or anxiety
  • Pre-workout drinks may not be necessary for everyone, especially those who have a balanced diet and are not looking to increase their athletic performance

Overall, pre-workout drinks can be a great addition to your morning routine if you’re looking to improve your athletic performance and fuel your body properly. However, it’s important to choose a drink that works for your body and goals, and to not rely too heavily on supplements or drinks to improve your workout.
