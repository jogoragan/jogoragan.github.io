How to Watch the Interview

The Good Morning America Harry interview will air on ABC at 7:00 a.m. ET on Thursday, March 9, 2023. You can watch it on your television if you have cable or satellite, or you can stream it online.

To stream the interview online, you can use the ABC app or website. Here's how:

  1. Go to the ABC website or download the ABC app on your device.
  2. Sign in with your cable or satellite provider information.
  3. Navigate to the Good Morning America section and find the Harry interview.
  4. Click on the video to start watching.

That's it! You should now be able to watch the interview.

Understanding the Interview

If English isn't your first language, you may find it difficult to understand the interview. Prince Harry has a British accent, and he may use some words or phrases that you're not familiar with. Additionally, the interviewers may speak quickly or use idioms that you don't understand.

However, you can still enjoy the interview and understand the main points. Here are some tips:

Listen Carefully

Pay attention to the words that are being spoken. If you don't understand something, rewind and listen again. Try to pick up on the main ideas and ignore the details that you don't understand.

Watch the Body Language

Prince Harry's body language can give you clues about what he's saying. Watch his facial expressions and gestures to get a better understanding of his emotions and thoughts.

Read the Subtitles

If you're watching the interview online, you may have the option to turn on subtitles. This can help you understand the words that are being spoken.

Take Notes

If you're really struggling to understand the interview, take notes on the main ideas. This can help you remember what's being said and give you a better understanding of the overall message.

Tips for Understanding

Here are some additional tips for understanding the interview:

Learn English

If you're not fluent in English, consider taking classes or using language learning apps to improve your skills. The more you practice, the easier it will be to understand English speakers.

Watch with a Friend

If you have a friend who is fluent in English, watch the interview together. They can help you understand any difficult words or phrases, and you can discuss the interview together.

Watch Other Interviews

If you're unfamiliar with British accents, watch other interviews with British people to get used to the way they speak. This can help you understand Prince Harry's accent better.

FAQ

Can I watch the interview on YouTube?

It's possible that someone will upload the interview to YouTube after it airs, but we don't recommend relying on this. It's better to watch the interview live or on a reputable streaming service.

Will there be subtitles available?

It's possible that subtitles will be available, but this depends on the streaming service you're using. Check the settings to see if you can turn on subtitles.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • You can watch the interview from the comfort of your own home.
  • You can pause and rewind if you need to.
  • You can take notes to help you remember important points.

Cons

  • If you're not fluent in English, you may have difficulty understanding the interview.
  • The interview may not be available in your country.
  • You may need a cable or satellite subscription to watch the interview.

Overall, watching the Good Morning America Harry interview can be a fun and informative experience. By following our tips, you can improve your understanding of the interview and enjoy it even if English isn't your first language.
