Things To Do At Night In Ubud A Guide To Relaxing Evenings

If you're looking for a peaceful and serene getaway in Bali, Ubud is the perfect place to visit. Known for its lush greenery, peaceful surroundings, and cultural offerings, Ubud offers a variety of activities to enjoy during the day. However, the fun doesn't end when the sun sets. Ubud also has a lot to offer when it comes to nighttime entertainment. In this post, we'll cover some of the best things to do at night in Ubud.

1. Watch Traditional Balinese Dance Performances

One of the best ways to experience the local culture is by watching traditional Balinese dance performances. There are several places in Ubud where you can catch a live performance, such as the Ubud Palace and Ubud Art Market. These shows usually start around 7 PM and last for an hour or two. The performances are accompanied by traditional Balinese music and colorful costumes, making for a mesmerizing experience.

2. Explore the Night Market

Ubud Night Market is a must-visit for foodies. The market is open every night and offers a variety of local delicacies, such as nasi goreng, satay, and bakso. You can also find fresh fruits, vegetables, and handmade crafts at the market. The atmosphere is lively and bustling, making it a great place to soak up the local culture.

3. Enjoy a Delicious Dinner

Ubud has no shortage of great restaurants that offer delicious food and drinks. Whether you're in the mood for Indonesian cuisine, vegan fare, or international dishes, you'll find plenty of options to choose from. Some of the best restaurants in Ubud include Locavore, Hujan Locale, and Swept Away at The Samaya.

4. Get a Relaxing Spa Treatment

Ubud is known for its luxurious spas that offer a range of treatments, from traditional Balinese massages to aromatherapy facials. If you want to relax and unwind after a long day of exploring, head to one of the many spas in Ubud. Some of the best ones include Karsa Spa, Taksu Spa, and Karsa Kafe.

5. Attend a Yoga Class

Yoga is a popular activity in Ubud, and there are many studios that offer classes for all levels. Practicing yoga in Ubud is a great way to connect with your body and mind while surrounded by the beautiful nature. Some of the best yoga studios in Ubud include Yoga Barn, Radiantly Alive, and Ubud Yoga Centre.

6. Join a Meditation Session

In addition to yoga classes, many of the yoga studios and spas in Ubud also offer meditation sessions. Meditation is a great way to calm your mind and reduce stress. You can also learn different techniques and practices to incorporate into your daily routine.

7. Take a Nighttime Walk through the Rice Fields

Rice paddies are one of the most iconic images of Bali, and Ubud is surrounded by them. Taking a stroll through the rice fields at night is a unique experience that you won't forget. You can also book a guided tour to learn more about the local agriculture and culture.

8. Visit a Local Art Gallery

Ubud is home to many art galleries and museums that showcase traditional and contemporary Balinese art. Visiting a gallery at night is a great way to avoid the crowds and appreciate the artwork in a peaceful setting. Some of the best galleries in Ubud include Museum Puri Lukisan, Neka Art Museum, and Agung Rai Museum of Art.

9. Attend a Cooking Class

Balinese cuisine is known for its rich flavors and unique spices. If you want to learn how to cook traditional Balinese dishes, attend a cooking class in Ubud. You'll get to learn about the ingredients, techniques, and cultural significance of the dishes. Some of the best cooking classes in Ubud include Casa Luna Cooking School and Paon Bali Cooking Class.

10. Hang Out in a Cozy Café

Ubud has many cozy cafés and bars where you can relax and unwind with a cup of coffee or a cocktail. Some of the best ones include Clear Cafe, Seniman Coffee Studio, and Kafe Ubud. You can also find live music performances and open mic nights at some of the bars.

Now that you know some of the best things to do at night in Ubud, you can plan your itinerary accordingly. Whether you want to immerse yourself in the local culture or simply relax and unwind, Ubud has something for everyone.
