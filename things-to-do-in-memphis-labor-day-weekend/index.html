Are you looking for a fun and exciting way to spend your Labor Day weekend in Memphis? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the top things to do in Memphis during the holiday weekend. From outdoor activities to cultural experiences, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Table of Contents

  • Outdoor Activities
  • Cultural Experiences

Outdoor Activities

Memphis is home to some great outdoor activities, perfect for enjoying the long holiday weekend.

Visit Shelby Farms Park

Shelby Farms Park is a 4,500-acre park with trails, lakes, and playgrounds. It's the perfect place to spend a day outdoors with family and friends. You can rent bikes, kayaks, and paddleboards, or just enjoy a picnic in the park.

Go on a Riverboat Cruise

Take a relaxing riverboat cruise down the Mississippi River and enjoy the beautiful views of Memphis from the water. Memphis Riverboats offers several different cruise options, including dinner cruises and sightseeing tours.

Cultural Experiences

Memphis is a city rich in history and culture, with plenty of opportunities to explore and learn during Labor Day weekend.

Visit the National Civil Rights Museum

The National Civil Rights Museum is located at the site of the Lorraine Motel, where Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. was assassinated. The museum offers a comprehensive look at the Civil Rights Movement and its impact on American history.

Explore the Memphis Zoo

The Memphis Zoo is home to over 3,500 animals from around the world. In addition to the animal exhibits, the zoo also offers educational programs and special events throughout the year.

Food and Drink

Memphis is known for its delicious food and drink scene, with plenty of options to satisfy your taste buds during Labor Day weekend.

Try Some Memphis BBQ

No trip to Memphis is complete without trying some of the city's famous BBQ. Check out Central BBQ or The Bar-B-Q Shop for some of the best BBQ in town.

Visit a Local Brewery

Memphis has a growing craft beer scene, with several local breweries to choose from. Check out Wiseacre Brewing Company or Ghost River Brewing for some great beer and a fun atmosphere.

Family-Friendly Activities

If you're traveling with kids, there are plenty of family-friendly activities to keep everyone entertained during Labor Day weekend.

Visit the Children's Museum of Memphis

The Children's Museum of Memphis offers hands-on exhibits and activities for kids of all ages. From a mini supermarket to a kid-sized fire station, there's plenty to explore and learn.

Watch a Redbirds Game

The Memphis Redbirds are a minor league baseball team with a fun and family-friendly atmosphere. Catch a game at AutoZone Park and enjoy some peanuts and Cracker Jacks.

Nightlife

Memphis has a vibrant nightlife scene, with plenty of options for live music and entertainment during Labor Day weekend.

Listen to Live Music on Beale Street

Beale Street is known for its live music scene, with several bars and clubs featuring local musicians. Check out the Rum Boogie Cafe or B.B. King's Blues Club for some great music and a fun atmosphere.

Visit a Speakeasy

Memphis has several hidden speakeasies, perfect for a night out with friends. Check out The Blind Bear or Alchemy for some delicious cocktails and a unique atmosphere.

With so many things to do in Memphis during Labor Day weekend, you're sure to have a great time. Whether you're exploring the outdoors, learning about the city's history, or enjoying some delicious food and drink, there's something for everyone in Memphis.
