Welcome to Vancouver, the city of rain! With an average of 161 rainy days a year, it's important to have some indoor activities planned for those gloomy days. In this guide, we'll show you the best places to go and things to do when it's raining in Vancouver. Whether you're a local or a tourist, it's always good to have a backup plan for those wet days. So, let's get started!

Visit a Museum

Vancouver has a variety of museums to choose from, which makes for a perfect indoor activity on a rainy day. Some of the most popular museums are the Museum of Anthropology, The Vancouver Art Gallery, and the Science World. The Museum of Anthropology has a great collection of First Nations art and artifacts, while the Vancouver Art Gallery features a collection of contemporary art. Science World is perfect for families with kids as it has interactive exhibits and a large IMAX theatre.

Explore the Vancouver Aquarium

The Vancouver Aquarium is a great spot to explore on a rainy day. Located in Stanley Park, it is home to over 50,000 animals including dolphins, sea otters, and beluga whales. There are also interactive exhibits and daily shows featuring sea lions and dolphins. The aquarium is a fun and educational experience for all ages.

Relax at a Spa

What better way to spend a rainy day than relaxing at a spa? Vancouver has a variety of spas to choose from, ranging from luxury spas to affordable day spas. Some popular options are the Willow Stream Spa at the Fairmont Pacific Rim, the Scandinave Spa, and the Miraj Hammam Spa. A day at the spa is the perfect way to unwind and forget about the rain outside.

Go Shopping

Vancouver has a great selection of shopping destinations, which makes it perfect for a rainy day activity. Some of the most popular shopping spots are Pacific Centre, Robson Street, and Oakridge Centre. Pacific Centre is located in the heart of downtown and features over 100 stores including high-end fashion brands. Robson Street is known for its boutique shops and trendy cafes. Oakridge Centre is a bit further out but features luxury brands and a large food court.

Catch a Show at the Theater

Vancouver has a great theater scene, with many productions taking place throughout the year. The most popular theaters are the Queen Elizabeth Theatre, The Stanley Theatre, and The Arts Club Theatre Company. The Queen Elizabeth Theatre hosts large-scale productions such as Broadway shows and operas. The Stanley Theatre is a smaller venue but features a variety of plays and musicals. The Arts Club Theatre Company is a non-profit organization that produces a range of productions from classic plays to contemporary musicals.

Sip Coffee at a Cozy Cafe

Vancouver has a great selection of cozy cafes to choose from, which makes it perfect for a rainy day activity. Some of the most popular cafes are the Revolver Coffee, Matchstick Coffee Roasters, and Timbertrain Coffee Roasters. Revolver Coffee is known for its high-quality coffee and minimalist decor. Matchstick Coffee Roasters features a variety of baked goods and sandwiches. Timbertrain Coffee Roasters has a great ambiance and features unique coffee blends.

Hit the Gym

What better way to beat the rainy day blues than by hitting the gym? Vancouver has a variety of gyms to choose from, ranging from budget-friendly options to luxury gyms. Some popular options are the Steve Nash Fitness World, the Club16 Trevor Linden Fitness, and the Equinox Fitness Club. A workout is the perfect way to boost your mood and get your endorphins flowing.

Visit the Library

The Vancouver Public Library is a great spot to spend a rainy day. Located in downtown Vancouver, it is the largest public library in the city. The library features a variety of books, magazines, and newspapers as well as a cafe and public computers. The library is the perfect spot to get lost in a good book or catch up on some work.

Check Out an Art Gallery

Vancouver has a great selection of art galleries to choose from, which makes it perfect for a rainy day activity. Some of the most popular galleries are the Contemporary Art Gallery, the Belkin Art Gallery, and the Equinox Gallery. The Contemporary Art Gallery features a variety of contemporary art and installations, while the Belkin Art Gallery features a collection of contemporary and historical art. The Equinox Gallery features a selection of modern and contemporary art.

Watch a Movie at the Cinema

Watching a movie is the perfect way to spend a rainy day. Vancouver has a variety of cinemas to choose from, ranging from large-scale theaters to small independent cinemas. Some popular options are the Cineplex Odeon, the Rio Theatre, and the Dunbar Theatre. The Cineplex Odeon features a variety of mainstream movies, while the Rio Theatre features unique events such as cult movie screenings and live comedy shows. The Dunbar Theatre is a smaller venue but features a variety of independent films.

Tips for Enjoying Vancouver in the Rain

Here are some tips for enjoying Vancouver in the rain:

  • Bring an umbrella and rain jacket
  • Wear waterproof shoes
  • Take public transit instead of walking
  • Bring a waterproof camera to capture the scenery
  • Try some of the city's famous rainy day comfort food like a bowl of hot soup or a grilled cheese sandwich

Conclusion

Now that you know the best places to go in rainy weather in Vancouver, you can enjoy the city no matter what the weather is like. From museums to spas to cozy cafes, there's something for everyone to enjoy on a rainy day. So, grab your umbrella and explore all that Vancouver has to offer!
