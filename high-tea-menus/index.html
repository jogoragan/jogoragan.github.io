High tea menus are a wonderful way to enjoy an afternoon with friends and family. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just looking for a relaxing afternoon out, high tea menus offer a variety of delicious treats to enjoy. In this guide, we’ll take a look at what high tea is, what you can expect to find on a high tea menu, and some tips for making the most of your experience.

What is High Tea?

High tea is a traditional British meal that is typically served in the late afternoon or early evening. It usually consists of a selection of sandwiches, pastries, scones, and tea. The term “high tea” originated in the 19th century when the upper class would have a meal of tea, bread, butter, and cake after a day of hunting or other outdoor activities. Today, high tea has become a popular social activity and can be found in many hotels, tea rooms, and restaurants.

What to Expect on a High Tea Menu

High tea menus can vary depending on the establishment, but there are a few items that you can typically expect to find:

Tea

Of course, no high tea would be complete without tea! You’ll usually be given a selection of teas to choose from, such as Earl Grey, English Breakfast, or Darjeeling.

Sandwiches

High tea sandwiches are typically small, crustless sandwiches that are easy to eat with your fingers. You might find cucumber and cream cheese, smoked salmon and cream cheese, or egg salad sandwiches on the menu.

Scones

Scones are a staple of high tea menus. They’re usually served with clotted cream and jam, and are a sweet and filling treat.

Pastries and Cakes

High tea menus often include a selection of pastries and cakes. You might find macarons, lemon tarts, chocolate cake, or fruit tarts on the menu.

How to Enjoy High Tea

Here are a few tips for enjoying your high tea experience:

Dress Nicely

High tea is a special occasion, so dress nicely. You don’t need to wear a suit or a fancy dress, but avoid wearing jeans and t-shirts.

Arrive on Time

High tea is usually served at a specific time, so be sure to arrive on time. This will ensure that you don’t miss any of the delicious treats!

Take Your Time

High tea is meant to be a leisurely experience, so take your time and enjoy each item on the menu. Savor the flavors and enjoy the company of your friends and family.

Try Something New

High tea menus often include items that you might not normally try. Be adventurous and try something new!

Pros and Cons of High Tea Menus

Pros:

  • High tea is a fun and relaxing way to spend an afternoon
  • You get to enjoy a variety of delicious treats
  • It’s a great way to socialize with friends and family

Cons:

  • High tea can be expensive
  • It’s not always easy to find a high tea menu that accommodates dietary restrictions
  • It can be difficult to find availability, especially during peak hours or on weekends

FAQ

What should I wear to high tea?

You don’t need to wear a suit or a fancy dress, but avoid wearing jeans and t-shirts. Dress nicely and you’ll fit right in.

Do I need to make a reservation for high tea?

It’s always a good idea to make a reservation for high tea, especially during peak hours or on weekends.

Can I bring my own tea?

It’s best to check with the establishment beforehand to see if they allow outside tea.

Are high tea menus available for children?

Some establishments offer a children’s high tea menu, but it’s best to check beforehand.

Conclusion

High tea menus are a wonderful way to spend an afternoon with friends and family. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just looking for a relaxing afternoon out, high tea menus offer a variety of delicious treats to enjoy. Remember to dress nicely, arrive on time, and take your time enjoying each item on the menu. And don’t forget to try something new!
