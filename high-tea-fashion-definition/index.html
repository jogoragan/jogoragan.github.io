If you're planning to attend a high tea party or event, you might be wondering what to wear. High tea fashion is all about looking chic and sophisticated while enjoying tea and delicious treats. In this article, we'll go over the high tea fashion definition and give you some tips on how to dress for the occasion.

What is High Tea Fashion?

High tea is a British tradition that dates back to the 19th century. It originated as a meal for the working class, but soon became a fashionable event for the upper class. High tea is served late in the afternoon and consists of tea, sandwiches, scones, and sweets.

High tea fashion refers to the attire worn by guests attending a high tea event. It's often described as smart casual, which means it's more formal than casual wear but not as dressy as formal wear.

How to Dress for High Tea: Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to dress for a high tea event:

Step 1: Check the Invitation

The first thing you should do is check the invitation. Some high tea events may have a dress code, which will give you an idea of what to wear. If there's no dress code specified, you can assume that it's smart casual.

Step 2: Choose Your Outfit

When choosing your outfit, keep in mind that high tea is a formal event. Women should wear a dress or a skirt and blouse. Men should wear slacks and a dress shirt. Avoid wearing jeans, shorts, or t-shirts.

Step 3: Choose Your Shoes

For women, high heels or dressy flats are appropriate. Men should wear dress shoes. Avoid wearing sneakers or sandals.

Step 4: Accessorize

Accessorize your outfit with jewelry, a scarf, or a hat. Keep it simple and elegant. Avoid wearing anything too flashy or distracting.

Tips for High Tea Fashion

Here are some tips to keep in mind when dressing for a high tea event:

  • Wear light-colored clothes for daytime events and darker colors for evening events.
  • Avoid wearing anything too revealing or tight-fitting.
  • Keep your makeup and hair simple and elegant.
  • Wear comfortable shoes, as you'll be on your feet for a while.
  • Bring a small purse or clutch to carry your essentials.

FAQ

What should men wear to high tea?

Men should wear slacks and a dress shirt. Avoid wearing jeans, shorts, or t-shirts. Dress shoes are also recommended.

What should women wear to high tea?

Women should wear a dress or a skirt and blouse. High heels or dressy flats are appropriate. Avoid wearing jeans, shorts, or t-shirts.

Can I wear jeans to high tea?

No, jeans are not appropriate for a high tea event. Stick to more formal attire such as a dress or slacks and a dress shirt.

Pros and Cons of High Tea Fashion

Here are some pros and cons of dressing for a high tea event:

Pros

  • You'll look chic and sophisticated.
  • You'll fit in with the formal atmosphere.

Cons

  • You may have to purchase new clothing if you don't already own appropriate attire.
  • You may feel uncomfortable if you're not used to dressing formally.

Overall, high tea fashion is all about looking stylish, sophisticated, and appropriate for the occasion. Follow our tips and you'll be sure to make a great impression at your next high tea event.
