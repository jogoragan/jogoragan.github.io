Things To Do On A Rainy Day In Kansas City

Written by April Mar 06, 2023 · 3 min read

When the skies are gray and the rain is pouring, it can be easy to feel cooped up inside. However, Kansas City has plenty of indoor activities to keep you entertained on a rainy day. In this guide, we'll share some of our favorite things to do in Kansas City when the weather isn't cooperating.

Visit a Museum

Kansas City has a variety of museums to choose from, including the National World War I Museum and Memorial, the Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art, and the Arabia Steamboat Museum. Spend some time learning about history, art, or science while staying dry inside.

Catch a Movie

There are plenty of movie theaters in Kansas City, including the Alamo Drafthouse, the Screenland Armour, and the AMC Ward Parkway. Grab some popcorn and enjoy the latest blockbuster or indie flick.

Play Arcade Games

Take a trip down memory lane and play some classic arcade games at Up-Down, a retro arcade bar. With over 50 games to choose from and craft beer on tap, you can easily spend an afternoon here.

Go Bowling

Head to Lucky Strike in the Power & Light District for a few rounds of bowling. They also have billiards, shuffleboard, and tasty food and drinks to keep you entertained.

Indoor Shopping

If you're in the mood to shop, check out the Country Club Plaza or the Crown Center. Both offer indoor shopping options and a variety of stores to choose from.

Visit an Aquarium

The SEA LIFE Kansas City Aquarium is a great option for families or anyone who loves marine life. You can get up close and personal with sharks, rays, and other sea creatures without getting wet.

Visit a Library

The Kansas City Public Library has several branches throughout the city, each with its own unique architecture and design. Spend some time browsing the stacks, attending a workshop or event, or just enjoying the peaceful atmosphere.

Escape Room Adventures

If you're looking for a fun and challenging activity, try an escape room. Escape KC and Breakout KC offer different themed rooms to choose from and a chance to test your problem-solving skills.

Explore Art Galleries

The Crossroads Arts District is home to several art galleries and studios, making it a great place to spend a rainy day. Check out the latest exhibits and support local artists.

Try New Food

Kansas City is known for its barbecue, but there are plenty of other food options to explore as well. Try a new restaurant or cuisine, or take a food tour to sample a variety of flavors.

Tips for a Rainy Day in Kansas City

When planning your rainy day activities in Kansas City, keep these tips in mind:

  • Check the weather forecast ahead of time and plan accordingly.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and bring an umbrella or rain jacket.
  • Consider purchasing tickets or making reservations in advance to avoid long lines or wait times.
  • Don't let the rain dampen your spirits – embrace the opportunity to try something new or explore a different part of the city.

Conclusion

Rainy days don't have to be boring in Kansas City. With these indoor activities and attractions, you can stay entertained and dry while waiting for the sun to come back out. Whether you're in the mood for art, history, or just some good food, there's something for everyone to enjoy.
