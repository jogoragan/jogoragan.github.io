What To Do In 2 Days In Dubai

If you're planning to visit Dubai but only have two days to explore, it's essential to plan your itinerary carefully to make the most of your trip. With so many things to see and do, it can be challenging to decide which activities and attractions to prioritize. Here's a guide to help you plan your two-day itinerary in Dubai.

Day 1

Visit The Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, standing at a height of 828 meters. The observation deck on the 124th floor offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city. You can also catch the light and water show at the Dubai Fountain, which is located at the base of the Burj Khalifa.

Explore Dubai Mall

Dubai Mall is one of the largest shopping malls in the world, with more than 1,200 stores. You can find everything from luxury designer brands to high-street fashion. The mall also features an indoor aquarium and underwater zoo, a virtual reality park, an ice rink, and a cinema.

Experience The Dubai Miracle Garden

The Dubai Miracle Garden is a stunning floral attraction that features more than 45 million flowers arranged in various shapes and designs. It's the perfect place to take beautiful photos and enjoy the colorful scenery.

Day 2

Take A Dubai City Tour

If you want to explore the city and learn more about its history and culture, taking a city tour is a great option. You can visit the Dubai Museum, Jumeirah Mosque, and the old Dubai Creek area. You can also take a traditional boat ride, known as an abra, across the creek.

Visit The Palm Jumeirah

The Palm Jumeirah is an artificial island that resembles a palm tree. It features luxurious hotels, restaurants, and entertainment options. You can take a monorail ride to explore the island, visit the Atlantis Aquaventure Waterpark, or relax on one of the beaches.

Experience The Dubai Desert

A trip to Dubai wouldn't be complete without experiencing the desert. You can take a desert safari, which includes a thrilling dune bashing ride, camel riding, and a traditional Arabic dinner under the stars. You can also watch a belly dancing and fire show.

Tips

It's essential to dress appropriately when visiting Dubai, especially when visiting religious sites. Women should cover their shoulders and knees, and men should avoid wearing shorts. It's also important to respect local customs and traditions.

Make sure to stay hydrated, especially during the summer months, as temperatures can reach up to 50 degrees Celsius. It's also advisable to wear sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.

FAQ

What is the best time to visit Dubai?

The best time to visit Dubai is between November and April when the weather is pleasant and mild. The summer months can be extremely hot and humid, making it difficult to enjoy outdoor activities.

Is Dubai safe for tourists?

Yes, Dubai is generally a safe city for tourists. However, it's essential to be aware of your surroundings and avoid carrying large amounts of cash or valuables.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Dubai offers a wide range of attractions and activities.
  • The city is safe and well-maintained.
  • The public transport system is efficient and affordable.

Cons:

  • Visiting Dubai can be expensive, especially if you're interested in luxury experiences.
  • The summer months can be extremely hot and humid.
  • The city can be crowded, especially during peak tourist season.

Overall, Dubai is a fascinating city to explore, and two days is enough time to experience some of its most iconic attractions. Whether you're interested in shopping, sightseeing, or adventure, there's something for everyone in Dubai.
