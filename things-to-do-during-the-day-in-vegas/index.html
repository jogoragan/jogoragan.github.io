Things To Do During The Day In Vegas

Written by Alvine Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

.

If you're planning a trip to Las Vegas, you're probably wondering what there is to do during the day. While Vegas is known for its nightlife, there are plenty of activities to keep you busy during the day. In this post, we'll explore some of the best things to do during the day in Vegas, from sightseeing to shopping to outdoor activities.

Table of Contents

  • Sightseeing
  • Shopping
  • Outdoor Activities
  • Museums and Exhibits
  • Food and Drink

Sightseeing

One of the most popular things to do in Vegas during the day is sightseeing. The city is home to a number of iconic landmarks and attractions, including the Las Vegas Strip, the Bellagio Fountains, and the High Roller Observation Wheel. You can also take a helicopter tour of the Grand Canyon or explore the Hoover Dam. If you're interested in history, check out the Neon Museum or the Mob Museum.

Pro Tip:

If you plan on doing a lot of sightseeing, consider purchasing a Las Vegas Explorer Pass. This pass gives you access to multiple attractions at a discounted price.

Shopping

Vegas is also a shopper's paradise, with a variety of malls and shopping centers to choose from. If you're looking for high-end designer brands, head to the Fashion Show Mall or the Forum Shops at Caesars Palace. For more budget-friendly options, check out the Miracle Mile Shops or the Las Vegas North Premium Outlets.

Pro Tip:

Many shopping centers offer free shuttle services to and from your hotel. Check with your hotel concierge for more information.

Outdoor Activities

While Vegas is located in the middle of the desert, there are still plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. You can take a hot air balloon ride over the city, go horseback riding in Red Rock Canyon, or take a guided ATV tour through the desert. For a more relaxing experience, check out the pools at some of the city's top hotels.

Pro Tip:

Be sure to wear sunscreen and drink plenty of water if you're participating in outdoor activities. The desert sun can be brutal!

Museums and Exhibits

If you're looking for a more educational experience, Vegas has a number of museums and exhibits to choose from. The Discovery Children's Museum is perfect for families with young children, while the Titanic Artifact Exhibition offers a glimpse into the tragic history of the ill-fated ship. For something a little more unusual, check out the Zak Bagans' Haunted Museum.

Pro Tip:

Many museums and exhibits offer discounted admission during certain times of the day or week. Check their websites for more information.

Food and Drink

Finally, no trip to Vegas would be complete without sampling some of the city's famous cuisine. From celebrity chef restaurants to hole-in-the-wall eateries, there's something for everyone. Be sure to try some of the city's signature dishes, like shrimp cocktail or a classic steakhouse meal.

Pro Tip:

Many restaurants offer special deals during happy hour or early bird hours. Check with the individual restaurants for more information.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do during the day in Vegas. Whether you're interested in sightseeing, shopping, outdoor activities, or museums, there's something for everyone. Just be sure to stay hydrated and wear sunscreen, and you'll have a great time exploring all that Vegas has to offer.
