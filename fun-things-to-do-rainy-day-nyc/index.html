Fun Things To Do On A Rainy Day In Nyc

Are you feeling stuck at home on a rainy day in New York City? Don't worry, there are plenty of fun things to do to pass the time. From indoor activities to cozy cafes, we've got you covered. Here are some of our top picks for fun things to do on a rainy day in NYC:

Visit a Museum

One of the best things to do on a rainy day is to visit a museum. NYC is home to some of the best museums in the world, including the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the Museum of Modern Art, and the American Museum of Natural History. Spend the day exploring art, history, and culture while staying dry indoors.

Go to the Movies

Another great indoor activity is going to the movies. NYC has plenty of theaters to choose from, including indie cinemas and big chains like AMC and Regal. Grab some popcorn and settle in for a matinee or evening showing of the latest blockbuster.

Shop at a Mall

On a rainy day, shopping at a mall can be a fun way to spend the day. Head to one of the many malls in the city, including the Westfield World Trade Center, The Shops at Columbus Circle, or the Queens Center Mall. Not only will you stay dry, but you'll also have plenty of options for food, drinks, and entertainment.

Visit an Aquarium

If you're looking for a unique indoor activity, consider visiting an aquarium. NYC is home to the New York Aquarium, which has over 350 species of marine animals. Spend the day learning about sea life and watching live animal feedings.

Have a Spa Day

On a rainy day, there's nothing better than pampering yourself with a spa day. Head to one of the many spas in the city, such as the Shibui Spa at The Greenwich Hotel or the Mandarin Oriental Spa. Indulge in a massage, facial, or other relaxing treatment.

Cozy up in a Cafe

When it's raining outside, there's nothing cozier than finding a warm cafe to relax in. NYC has plenty of cafes to choose from, including independent coffee shops and big chains like Starbucks and Dunkin' Donuts. Grab a book or a laptop and spend the day sipping coffee and people watching.

Visit a Bookstore

If you're a book lover, visiting a bookstore can be a fun way to spend a rainy day. NYC has plenty of independent bookstores to choose from, including The Strand and McNally Jackson Books. Browse the shelves and find your next favorite read.

Take a Cooking Class

On a rainy day, taking a cooking class can be a fun and educational activity. NYC has plenty of cooking schools and classes to choose from, including the Institute of Culinary Education and the Natural Gourmet Institute. Learn how to make a new dish or refine your culinary skills.

Play Board Games

When it's raining outside, playing board games can be a fun way to pass the time. Invite some friends over or head to a board game cafe like The Uncommons in Greenwich Village. Choose from a variety of games and enjoy some friendly competition.

Visit an Escape Room

If you're looking for a unique and thrilling indoor activity, consider visiting an escape room. NYC has plenty of escape rooms to choose from, including Komnata Quest and Escape Games NYC. Solve puzzles and work together to escape before time runs out.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of fun things to do on a rainy day in NYC. Whether you prefer indoor activities like museums and movies or cozy cafes and spas, there's something for everyone. Don't let the rain get you down - embrace it and have some fun!
