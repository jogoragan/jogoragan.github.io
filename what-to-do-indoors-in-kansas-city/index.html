Welcome to Kansas City! If you're visiting this city during the cold or rainy months or just need a break from the outdoors, don't worry! There are plenty of indoor activities to keep you entertained. This article will provide you with a list of the best things to do indoors in Kansas City in 2023.

1. Visit the National World War I Museum

The National World War I Museum is located in Kansas City and offers an educational and interactive experience for visitors. The museum features a variety of exhibits and artifacts that tell the story of World War I, including weapons, uniforms, and personal items from soldiers. Visitors can also explore the museum's trenches and see what life was like for soldiers during the war. This museum is a must-see for history buffs and anyone interested in learning more about this important time in world history.

2. Explore the Negro Leagues Baseball Museum

The Negro Leagues Baseball Museum celebrates the history of African American baseball players who were excluded from playing in the Major Leagues until 1947. The museum features exhibits and displays about the players, teams, and leagues that made up the Negro Leagues. Visitors can see artifacts such as jerseys, equipment, and photographs, as well as interactive exhibits that allow you to experience what it was like to play in the Negro Leagues. This museum is a great place to learn about an important part of baseball history that is often overlooked.

3. Take a Trip to the Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art

The Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art is one of the best art museums in the country and is a must-see for anyone visiting Kansas City. The museum features a wide variety of art from around the world and throughout history, including works by famous artists such as Monet, Van Gogh, and Rembrandt. The museum also has a large collection of Asian art and a beautiful sculpture garden. Admission to the museum is free, but donations are appreciated.

4. Enjoy an Afternoon at the SEA LIFE Aquarium

The SEA LIFE Aquarium is a great place to spend a day with the family. The aquarium features a variety of exhibits and displays that allow you to get up close and personal with marine life from around the world. Visitors can see sharks, stingrays, and sea turtles, as well as seahorses, jellyfish, and other colorful fish. The aquarium also has touch pools where you can interact with starfish and other sea creatures. This is a great place to learn about marine life and conservation efforts.

5. Have Fun at the LEGOLAND Discovery Center

The LEGOLAND Discovery Center is a fun and interactive experience for kids and adults who love LEGO. The center features a variety of LEGO-themed exhibits and activities, including a 4D cinema, a LEGO factory tour, and a LEGO-themed ride. Visitors can also build their own creations in the Master Builder Academy and see mini versions of famous landmarks made entirely out of LEGO. This is a great place to let your creativity run wild and have fun with the whole family.

6. Watch a Show at the Kauffman Center for the Performing Arts

The Kauffman Center for the Performing Arts is a beautiful venue that hosts a variety of shows and performances throughout the year. The center is home to the Kansas City Ballet, the Kansas City Symphony, and the Lyric Opera of Kansas City, as well as a variety of other touring acts. Visitors can enjoy ballets, operas, concerts, and other performances in a state-of-the-art facility that is sure to impress. This is a great place to see world-class performances and support the arts.

7. Discover Science at the Science City at Union Station

Science City at Union Station is a hands-on science center that is perfect for kids and adults who love to learn. The center features a variety of interactive exhibits and displays that allow you to explore science in a fun and engaging way. Visitors can learn about everything from space travel to electricity to weather patterns, and there are plenty of opportunities for hands-on experimentation. This is a great place to spark your curiosity and learn something new.

8. Go Shopping at the Country Club Plaza

The Country Club Plaza is a beautiful outdoor shopping district that features a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options. However, if the weather is bad, you can still enjoy shopping at the many indoor stores that are located throughout the plaza. Visitors can find everything from high-end designer boutiques to popular chain stores, as well as plenty of restaurants and cafes. This is a great place to shop, eat, and people-watch, even on a rainy day.

9. Experience Virtual Reality at VRKC

VRKC is a virtual reality center that allows you to experience the latest in VR technology. Visitors can choose from a variety of experiences, including games, simulations, and educational programs. The center uses state-of-the-art VR technology to create immersive and realistic experiences that are sure to impress. This is a great place to try something new and experience the future of entertainment.

10. Get Your Game On at Up-Down Arcade Bar

Up-Down Arcade Bar is a fun and funky bar that features a variety of classic arcade games, pinball machines, and other retro games. Visitors can enjoy a drink or snack while playing their favorite games from the past. The bar also features a variety of events and specials throughout the week, including trivia nights, karaoke, and themed parties. This is a great place to relive your childhood and have fun with friends.

Tips for Enjoying Your Indoor Activities in Kansas City

  • Make sure to check the hours and admission fees for each attraction before you go.
  • Some attractions may require advance reservations or have limited capacity, so plan ahead.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and dress appropriately for the weather if you plan on walking or exploring outdoors.
  • Bring a camera to capture your memories and experiences.
  • Consider purchasing a CityPASS if you plan on visiting multiple attractions, as it can save you money and time.

Conclusion

Kansas City is a great place to visit year-round, and there are plenty of indoor activities to keep you entertained during the colder months. Whether you're interested in history, art, science, or just having fun, there is something for everyone in this vibrant city. Use this list as a starting point for planning your indoor activities in Kansas City in 2023, and enjoy everything this city has to offer.
