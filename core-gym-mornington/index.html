What is Core Gym Mornington?

Core Gym Mornington is a locally owned and operated gym in Mornington, Victoria. The gym offers a range of fitness programs, including personal training, group fitness classes, and nutrition coaching. The gym is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, making it easy for you to fit a workout into your busy schedule.

How to Become a Member

Becoming a member of Core Gym Mornington is easy. Simply visit the gym's website and fill out a membership application. You will then be contacted by a member of the gym's staff to set up a tour of the gym and to discuss your fitness goals. Membership rates vary depending on the package you choose, but all memberships include access to the gym's equipment, group fitness classes, and personal training sessions.

Programs Available

Personal Training

Core Gym Mornington offers personal training sessions with certified trainers who will help you achieve your fitness goals. Personal training sessions can be scheduled at your convenience and are tailored specifically to your fitness level and goals. Whether you are looking to lose weight, build muscle, or improve your overall fitness, the trainers at Core Gym Mornington will help you get there.

Group Fitness Classes

Core Gym Mornington offers a variety of group fitness classes, including yoga, Pilates, spin, and HIIT. These classes are led by certified instructors who will guide you through a full-body workout. Group fitness classes are a great way to stay motivated and to meet new people who share your fitness goals.

Nutrition Coaching

Core Gym Mornington offers nutrition coaching to help you make healthy food choices and to develop a meal plan that supports your fitness goals. Nutrition coaching sessions can be scheduled at your convenience and are tailored specifically to your dietary needs and fitness goals.
