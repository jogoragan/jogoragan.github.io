Welcome to our guide on things to do Valentine's Day 2022 NYC. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide what to do on this special day. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner, a fun activity, or a unique experience, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Romantic Dinner

There's nothing more romantic than a candlelit dinner with your significant other. Here are some of the best restaurants in NYC that will make your Valentine's Day unforgettable:

1. Gramercy Tavern

If you're looking for a cozy and intimate atmosphere, Gramercy Tavern is the perfect spot. With a seasonal menu and an extensive wine list, this restaurant will impress your taste buds.

2. The River Café

Located under the Brooklyn Bridge, The River Café offers stunning views of the Manhattan skyline. The restaurant serves classic American cuisine and has an extensive wine list.

3. L'Artusi

If you're a fan of Italian food, L'Artusi is the place to be. The restaurant has a romantic ambiance and serves delicious pasta dishes.

Outdoor Activities

If you're looking for a more adventurous Valentine's Day, here are some outdoor activities you can enjoy in NYC:

1. Ice Skating at Rockefeller Center

What's more romantic than ice skating with your partner? Rockefeller Center is the perfect place to do so. With the iconic Christmas tree in the background, this activity will make your Valentine's Day unforgettable.

2. Central Park Horse Carriage Ride

Take a romantic horse carriage ride with your significant other in the heart of Central Park. Enjoy the views of the park while sipping on hot chocolate.

3. Brooklyn Botanic Garden

Take a stroll through the Brooklyn Botanic Garden and admire the beautiful flowers and plants. The garden is open year-round and has different exhibits depending on the season.

Indoor Activities

If you prefer to stay indoors, here are some activities that will make your Valentine's Day special:

1. Museum of Modern Art

The Museum of Modern Art is a perfect place to spend a romantic day with your partner. Admire the art and take a break at the museum's restaurant, The Modern.

2. Spa Day

Treat yourselves to a spa day and relax together. NYC has many spas that offer couples massages and treatments.

3. Broadway Show

Enjoy a Broadway show with your significant other. From romantic comedies to dramas, there's a show for everyone.

Unique Experiences

If you're looking for a unique Valentine's Day experience, here are some ideas:

1. Helicopter Tour

Take a helicopter tour of NYC and enjoy the views from above. This is a unique and unforgettable experience that you and your partner will never forget.

2. Sunset Sail

Enjoy a romantic sunset sail around the Statue of Liberty. This experience includes live music, drinks, and hors d'oeuvres.

3. Escape Room

Challenge yourselves to an escape room game. This activity is perfect for couples who love to solve puzzles and work together.

Tips for a Perfect Valentine's Day

Here are some tips to make your Valentine's Day in NYC perfect:

  • Make reservations in advance to avoid disappointment.
  • Dress appropriately for the activity or restaurant you're going to.
  • Take public transportation or hire a car to avoid traffic and parking problems.
  • Surprise your partner with a thoughtful gift.
  • Put away your phone and enjoy the moment with your significant other.

FAQs

1. What is the best restaurant for a romantic dinner in NYC?

Gramercy Tavern, The River Café, and L'Artusi are some of the best restaurants for a romantic dinner in NYC.

2. What are some unique experiences for Valentine's Day in NYC?

Helicopter tours, sunset sails, and escape room games are some unique experiences for Valentine's Day in NYC.

3. What should I wear for a Valentine's Day activity in NYC?

Dress appropriately for the activity or restaurant you're going to. If you're going ice skating, wear warm clothes. If you're going to a Broadway show, dress up.

We hope this guide on things to do Valentine's Day 2022 NYC has been helpful. Whether you're a local or a tourist, NYC has something for everyone. Have a happy Valentine's Day!
