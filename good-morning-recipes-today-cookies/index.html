Description

Good morning cookies are a perfect way to start off your day. These cookies are delicious and easy to make. The ingredients used in making them are readily available, and the procedure is quite simple. You can make these cookies in various flavors such as chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and peanut butter, among others.

How to make Good Morning Cookies

Making good morning cookies is quite easy. Here are the basic ingredients you will need:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips or any other flavor of your choice

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to make the perfect good morning cookies:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, mix the butter, sugar, and vanilla extract until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl and add them to the butter mixture.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix well.
  6. Add in the chocolate chips or any other flavor of your choice.
  7. Drop the dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the edges turn golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool for a few minutes before serving.

Tips for making Good Morning Cookies

Here are some tips to help you make the best good morning cookies:

  • Use room temperature butter for easy mixing.
  • Sift the flour to prevent lumps in the mixture.
  • Use parchment paper to prevent the cookies from sticking to the baking sheet.
  • Don't overmix the dough as this can lead to tough cookies.
  • Add in the chocolate chips or any other flavor of your choice to suit your taste.

Solution to common problems

Here are some common problems you might encounter when making good morning cookies, and how to solve them:

  • If your cookies come out too flat, try adding more flour to the mixture.
  • If your cookies are too hard, try reducing the baking time or temperature.
  • If your cookies don't spread out enough, try using less flour or adding more butter.
  • If your cookies are too crumbly, try adding more liquid ingredients such as eggs or milk.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about good morning cookies:

  • Q: Can I use margarine instead of butter?
  • A: Yes, you can use margarine instead of butter, but the flavor and texture may be different.
  • Q: Can I freeze the cookie dough?
  • A: Yes, you can freeze the cookie dough for up to three months. Just make sure to wrap it tightly.
  • Q: Can I use brown sugar instead of white sugar?
  • A: Yes, you can use brown sugar instead of white sugar, but the cookies may be denser and have a stronger flavor.

Pros and Cons

Here are some pros and cons of making good morning cookies:

  • Pros: Quick and easy to make, delicious, and customizable.
  • Cons: High in calories and sugar, may not be suitable for people with dietary restrictions.

Overall, good morning cookies are a great way to start your day. With a few simple ingredients and some basic cooking skills, you can make delicious cookies that everyone will love.
