Recess

Recess was an animated show that followed a group of friends as they navigated their way through elementary school. The show was known for its clever writing and relatable characters, and it quickly became a fan favorite.

Mighty Morphin Power Rangers

Mighty Morphin Power Rangers was a live-action show that followed a group of teenagers who had been chosen to defend the world from evil forces. The show was known for its over-the-top action sequences and colorful costumes, and it quickly became a cultural phenomenon.
