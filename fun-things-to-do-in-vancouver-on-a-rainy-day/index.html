Fun Things To Do In Vancouver On A Rainy Day

Written by Bobby Aug 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you visiting Vancouver and the weather forecast isn't looking too promising? Don't worry, there are plenty of fun indoor activities to keep you entertained on a rainy day. Here are some of the best things to do in Vancouver on a rainy day.

1. Visit the Vancouver Aquarium

The Vancouver Aquarium is a great place to spend a rainy day. You can explore the indoor exhibits and learn about marine life from around the world. You can also watch live shows and interact with the animals.

2. Go to the Museum of Anthropology

The Museum of Anthropology is a fascinating museum that showcases Indigenous art and culture from around the world. It's a great place to learn about the history and traditions of different cultures.

3. Take a Cooking Class

What better way to spend a rainy day than by learning how to cook some delicious food? There are plenty of cooking classes available in Vancouver, whether you're interested in Asian cuisine, Italian food, or something else entirely.

4. Relax in a Spa

A rainy day is the perfect opportunity to pamper yourself and relax in a spa. There are plenty of spas in Vancouver that offer a range of treatments, from massages to facials.

5. Visit the Vancouver Art Gallery

The Vancouver Art Gallery is a great place to view contemporary art from around the world. You can also attend talks and events, or take a guided tour of the exhibits.

6. Go to an Escape Room

If you're looking for a fun and challenging activity, try an escape room. You'll be locked in a room and have to solve puzzles and clues to escape before time runs out.

7. Watch a Movie

Going to the movies is always a classic rainy day activity. There are plenty of cinemas in Vancouver that show the latest releases, as well as classic films and indie flicks.

8. Play Board Games

Gather some friends and spend the day playing board games. You can visit a board game café, or bring your own games to a cozy indoor space.

9. Visit Science World

Science World is a great place to learn about science and technology. There are interactive exhibits and demonstrations that are fun for all ages.

10. Explore Granville Island

Granville Island is a popular destination in Vancouver, even on a rainy day. You can explore the indoor markets and shops, or visit the Granville Island Brewery for a beer tasting.

In Conclusion

There are plenty of fun things to do in Vancouver on a rainy day, from visiting museums and galleries to escaping into a spa or playing board games. The key is to embrace the weather and make the most of your indoor activities.
