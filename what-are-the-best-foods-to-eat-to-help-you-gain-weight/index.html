What Are The Best Foods To Eat To Help You Gain Weight

Written by Jordan May 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to gain weight, the first thing you need to do is increase your calorie intake. But, it's not just about eating more food - you need to focus on consuming the right types of food. In this article, we'll explore the best foods to eat to help you gain weight and provide some tips on how to incorporate them into your diet.

Protein

Protein is essential for building muscle, so it's important to consume enough if you want to gain weight. Good sources of protein include:

  • Chicken breast
  • Salmon
  • Eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Whey protein powder

Protein shakes can be a great way to increase your protein intake, especially if you're on the go and don't have time to cook a full meal. Just be sure to choose a high-quality protein powder without added sugars or artificial ingredients.

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates provide much-needed energy for your body, so they're an important part of any weight-gain diet. However, it's important to choose complex carbohydrates, which are more nutrient-dense and provide longer-lasting energy. Good sources of complex carbohydrates include:

  • Brown rice
  • Quinoa
  • Sweet potato
  • Whole wheat bread
  • Oats

Simple carbohydrates, on the other hand, should be avoided or consumed in moderation, as they provide quick energy but lack important nutrients. Examples of simple carbohydrates include candy, soda, and white bread.

Healthy Fats

Fat is often demonized in the media, but it's actually an important part of a healthy diet - especially if you're trying to gain weight. Healthy fats provide energy and help your body absorb important vitamins and minerals. Good sources of healthy fats include:

  • Avocado
  • Nuts and nut butters
  • Seeds, such as chia and flax
  • Olive oil
  • Fatty fish, such as salmon

Fruits and Vegetables

Fruits and vegetables should be a staple in any healthy diet, including one designed for weight gain. They provide important nutrients and fiber, which is essential for digestion. Some high-calorie fruits and vegetables include:

  • Bananas
  • Mangos
  • Potatoes
  • Peas
  • Corn

Just be sure to avoid consuming too much fruit juice, which can be high in sugar and lacking in fiber.

Dairy Products

Dairy products are a great source of calcium and protein, making them an important part of any weight-gain diet. Good choices include:

  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Yogurt
  • Cottage cheese

Snacks

Snacking can be a great way to increase your calorie intake throughout the day, especially if you have a fast metabolism. Some good high-calorie snack options include:

  • Nuts and seeds
  • Trail mix
  • Granola bars
  • Protein bars
  • Jerky

Tips for Incorporating Weight-Gain Foods

Here are some tips for incorporating weight-gain foods into your diet:

  • Eat more often: Try eating 5-6 small meals throughout the day, rather than 3 large meals. This can help you consume more calories without feeling too full.
  • Add healthy fats to your meals: Try drizzling olive oil over your vegetables or adding avocado to your sandwich.
  • Drink your calories: Smoothies and protein shakes can be a great way to increase your calorie intake without feeling too full.
  • Snack strategically: Keep high-calorie snacks on hand, such as nuts and seeds, and eat them between meals.

Potential Cons of a High-Calorie Diet

While a high-calorie diet can be beneficial for weight gain, there are some potential downsides to consider. These include:

  • Increased risk of heart disease and other health problems if you consume too much saturated fat and sugar.
  • Feeling too full or uncomfortable after meals if you eat too much at once.
  • Difficulty sticking to a high-calorie diet if you're used to eating smaller meals.

FAQ

Q: Do I need to eat a lot of junk food to gain weight?

A: No, it's important to focus on nutrient-dense foods, rather than junk food. While treats can be enjoyed in moderation, they shouldn't be the mainstay of your diet.

Q: How much weight can I expect to gain by changing my diet?

A: This can vary depending on your individual metabolism and exercise routine, but you can expect to gain 1-2 pounds per week if you're consuming enough calories.

Q: Do I need to exercise to gain weight?

A: Exercise can be helpful for building muscle and increasing your appetite, but it's not strictly necessary. If you're consuming enough calories, you should be able to gain weight without exercise.

In conclusion, gaining weight requires a strategic approach to your diet. By focusing on nutrient-dense foods - including protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, fruits and vegetables, and dairy products - and incorporating high-calorie snacks and drinks, you can increase your calorie intake and achieve your weight-gain goals. Just be sure to monitor your intake of saturated fat and sugar, and consult a healthcare professional if you have any concerns.
