"कोशिश करने वालों की हार नहीं होती"

This is a famous quote by Harivansh Rai Bachchan which means that those who make an effort never lose. This quote is a reminder that success is not easy, but it is achievable if you keep trying.

"हमेशा याद रखो कि आप खुद से बेहतर बन सकते हो"

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam inspires you to believe in yourself and your abilities. It tells you that you have the potential to be better than you are today.

"जब तुम अपने मकसद के लिए खड़े होते हो, तो पूरी कायनात तुम्हें उसे हासिल करने में मदद करती है"

This quote by Paulo Coelho means that when you stand up for your goals, the universe conspires to help you achieve them. It inspires you to have faith in yourself and your dreams.

"हमेशा से कुछ न कुछ सीखो और अपनी गलतियों से सीखो"

This quote by Bill Gates reminds you that failure is a part of success. It tells you to learn from your mistakes and keep moving forward.

"जब आपके सपने बड़े होते हैं, तो आपके दुःख और संघर्ष भी बड़े होते हैं"

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam reminds you that when you dream big, your struggles and hardships become bigger too. But it also inspires you to keep pushing forward towards your goals.

"सफलता के लिए समय, मेहनत और धैर्य तीनों ज़रूरी हैं"

This quote by Swami Vivekananda tells you that success requires time, hard work, and patience. It inspires you to be persistent and never give up.

"आज की कोशिश कल की सफलता का संकेत होती है"

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam reminds you that every effort you make today is a step towards your future success. It inspires you to work hard and stay focused on your goals.

"आप जो भी बनना चाहते हैं, उसमें सफल होने के लिए तैयार हो जाइए"

This quote by Abraham Lincoln inspires you to be ready to work hard and make sacrifices to achieve your goals. It tells you that success requires effort and dedication.

"खुश रहो, आप जो कर रहे हो उसमें सफलता ज़रूर मिलेगी"

This quote by Dale Carnegie reminds you that happiness and success go hand in hand. It inspires you to enjoy the journey towards your goals and have a positive attitude.

"ज़िन्दगी में दूसरों के लिए कुछ करना है तो सफल होना ज़रूरी है"

This quote by APJ Abdul Kalam inspires you to use your skills and abilities to make a positive impact on the world. It tells you that success is not just about personal achievement but also about making a difference.

Conclusion

These famous success motivational quotes for students in Hindi are a great source of inspiration and motivation. They remind you that success is possible if you have faith in yourself, work hard, and never give up. So, whenever you feel demotivated or stuck in your journey towards success, remember these quotes and keep moving forward.
