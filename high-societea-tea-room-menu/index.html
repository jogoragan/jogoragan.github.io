Welcome to High Societea Tea Room, where we offer a luxurious and relaxing atmosphere to enjoy your favorite tea and snacks with your friends and family. We take pride in our extensive menu of tea options and delectable treats that will satisfy any palate. In this article, we will guide you through our High Societea Tea Room menu, providing you with how-to's, step-by-step instructions, tips, solutions, FAQs, as well as the pros and cons of each item on our menu. Let's dive in!

What's on the Menu?

At High Societea Tea Room, we offer a wide variety of tea blends, sandwiches, scones, and desserts. Our menu is designed to cater to different tastes and dietary needs. Whether you prefer classic Earl Grey tea or something more exotic like our Lavender Earl Grey, we have it all. Our sandwiches range from traditional cucumber and cream cheese to vegetarian options like our Caprese sandwich. Our scones are freshly baked daily and are served with Devonshire cream and jam. Lastly, our desserts are a must-try. From our chocolate truffles to our macarons, we have something for everyone.

How to Choose Your Tea Blend

With so many tea blends on our menu, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to choose. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Consider your mood: If you're feeling relaxed, go for a herbal tea like Chamomile. If you need a pick-me-up, try our English Breakfast tea.
  • Pair your tea with your food: If you're having a sandwich, choose a tea that complements it. Our Darjeeling tea goes well with our chicken salad sandwich.
  • Ask our staff: Our staff is knowledgeable about our tea blends and can recommend one based on your preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Tea

If you're having tea at High Societea Tea Room, our staff will make it for you. However, if you want to make tea at home, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Warm up your teapot by rinsing it with hot water.
  3. Measure out your tea leaves. We recommend using 1 teaspoon of loose leaf tea per 8 ounces of water.
  4. Put the tea leaves in the teapot and pour the hot water over them.
  5. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Pour the tea through a strainer into your teacup and enjoy!

Tips for Enjoying Your Scones

Our scones are one of our most popular items on the menu. Here are some tips for enjoying them:

  • Break your scone into small pieces before adding cream and jam.
  • Spread the cream and jam evenly over your scone.
  • Pair your scone with a cup of tea.

Solution to Tea Stains

If you accidentally spill tea on your clothes, don't panic. Here's how to remove tea stains:

  1. Blot the stain with a clean cloth to remove excess liquid.
  2. Mix 1 tablespoon of white vinegar with 1/2 cup of water.
  3. Dip a clean cloth in the solution and dab it on the stain.
  4. Rinse the cloth with water and dab the stain again.
  5. Wash the garment as usual.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about our High Societea Tea Room menu:

  • Do you offer gluten-free options? Yes, we do. Please let us know when you make your reservation.
  • Can I bring my own tea? Unfortunately, we do not allow outside food and drinks.
  • Do you offer vegan options? Yes, we have a vegan sandwich option available.

Pros and Cons of High Societea Tea Room Menu

Here are some pros and cons of our menu:

Cons:

  • Reservations are required.

Overall, High Societea Tea Room is a must-visit for tea lovers and those looking for a luxurious and relaxing experience. Our menu offers something for everyone, and our staff is always happy to help you choose the perfect tea blend and food item. We hope to see you soon!
