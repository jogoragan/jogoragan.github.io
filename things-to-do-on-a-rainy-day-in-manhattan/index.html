Things To Do On A Rainy Day In Manhattan

Rainy days in Manhattan can be a bummer, especially if you had plans to explore the city. But don't worry, there are plenty of indoor activities to keep you entertained. Whether you're a tourist or a local, here are some ideas on how to spend a rainy day in Manhattan.

Visit a Museum

Manhattan is home to some of the world's best museums, and they are perfect for a rainy day. The Metropolitan Museum of Art, the American Museum of Natural History, and the Museum of Modern Art are just a few of the options. You can spend hours exploring the exhibits, learning something new, and staying dry.

Go to a Broadway Show

Rainy days are the perfect excuse to see a Broadway show. There are plenty of theaters in Manhattan, and you're sure to find a show that fits your interests. From musicals to dramas, there's something for everyone. Plus, you'll be able to escape the rain and enjoy some world-class entertainment.

Shop at a Mall

If you're in the mood for some retail therapy, head to one of Manhattan's indoor malls. The Shops at Columbus Circle, Brookfield Place, and the Westfield World Trade Center are all great options. You can shop for clothes, accessories, and souvenirs, and grab a bite to eat at one of the many restaurants.

Check Out a Bookstore

Manhattan is home to some of the best independent bookstores in the country. Strand Bookstore, McNally Jackson, and Housing Works Bookstore Cafe are just a few of the options. You can browse the shelves, find a new book to read, and enjoy a cozy atmosphere.

Try a New Restaurant

Rainy days are the perfect excuse to indulge in some comfort food. Manhattan has no shortage of amazing restaurants, from pizza to ramen to burgers. Try something new and cozy up with a warm meal.

Visit an Art Gallery

If you're in the mood for something a little more low-key, check out one of Manhattan's many art galleries. There are galleries all over the city, featuring everything from contemporary art to classic paintings. You can wander through the exhibits and discover new artists.

Take a Cooking Class

Why not spend a rainy day learning a new skill? Manhattan has plenty of cooking classes, ranging from beginner to advanced. You can learn how to make pasta, sushi, or even chocolate. Plus, you'll get to enjoy the fruits of your labor.

Go to a Movie

Going to the movies is a classic rainy day activity. Manhattan has plenty of theaters, ranging from big chains to independent cinemas. You can catch a new release or a classic film, and enjoy some popcorn and candy.

Visit an Aquarium

If you're looking for something a little more unique, visit the New York Aquarium. You can see all sorts of sea creatures, from sharks to sea turtles. Plus, it's all indoors, so you'll stay dry.

Relax at a Spa

Finally, why not spend a rainy day relaxing at a spa? Manhattan has plenty of options, ranging from luxurious spas to more affordable ones. You can get a massage, a facial, or even a manicure. It's the perfect way to escape the rain and unwind.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do on a rainy day in Manhattan. Whether you want to explore a museum, see a show, or indulge in some comfort food, you'll find something to keep you entertained. So don't let the rain dampen your spirits - get out there and have some fun!
